here's a list of my favorite figma resources:

- [expo app icon & splash](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1155362909441341285): a template for creating consistent mobile app icons and splash screens.

- [ios 17 + ipados 17 ui kit](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1248375255495415511): a comprehensive ui kit following apple's latest design guidelines.

- [app store & google play download buttons](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1359492696316450448): a collection of official app store and google play store download buttons in various styles and languages.

how to design a mobile app?

open [ios 17 + ipados 17 ui kit](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1248375255495415511) in figma
select frame and pick presentation 16:9
add an iphone frame inside the presentation frame
round it up and add some bottom shadow
